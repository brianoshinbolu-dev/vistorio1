About Vistorio

Vistorio is your all-in-one desktop software for Hotels, Guest Houses, and Resorts. From managing guest check-ins to inventory, bar, and diesel supply records — Vistorio helps you operate smarter.

Key Features

Front Desk

Manage guest check-ins, room availability, and bookings with ease.

Bar & Restaurant POS

Sales tracking, receipt printing, and kitchen order routing.

Inventory Control

Real-time stock levels, requisitions, and low stock alerts.

Diesel Tracker

Track diesel deliveries, consumption, and logs efficiently.

Operations Reports

Get daily summaries, revenue reports, and staff logs.

Staff Access Control

Role-based access, audit trails, and shift logs.
